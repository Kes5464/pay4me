Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process transactions?
All transactions are processed instantly. You should receive confirmation within seconds.
What should I do if my transaction fails?
If a transaction fails, please check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, contact our support team with your transaction reference number.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect all your payment information. Your data is safe with us.
Can I get a refund for a failed transaction?
Yes, if you were debited for a failed transaction, please contact us with your transaction reference. Refunds are processed within 24-48 hours.
Which networks do you support?
We support MTN, Glo, Airtel, and 9Mobile for both airtime and data services.
How do I check my transaction history?
Login to your account and navigate to the transaction history section to view all your past transactions.
